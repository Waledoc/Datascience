❓ Question 1: How Do Weather Conditions Explain Renewable Generation?

Research Question

How strongly do weather conditions (especially wind and sunshine) explain daily electricity generation from wind (onshore/offshore) and PV in Germany?

R² Analysis: Weather's Explanatory Power

R² measures how well weather variables explain generation (0 = no explanation, 1 = perfect explanation)

Interactive: Wind Speed vs Generation

Interactive: Sunshine vs Solar Generation

💡 Key Insights

  • Wind Generation: Wind speed explains 50-70% of wind generation variation (R² ≈ 0.6)
  • Solar Generation: Sunshine duration explains 80%+ of solar generation (R² ≈ 0.85)
  • Non-linear Relationship: Wind generation shows exponential relationship with wind speed due to turbine power curves
  • Solar Predictability: Solar is more predictable due to direct sunshine → electricity conversion
