❓ Question 3: How Accurate Are Day-Ahead Forecasts?

Research Question

How accurate are SMARD day-ahead forecasts for wind and PV compared to actual generation — and does the forecast error vary by season?

Interactive: Seasonal Forecast Accuracy

Forecast vs Actual: Wind Generation

💡 Key Insights

  • Winter Variability: Wind forecasts have higher error in winter due to extreme weather events
  • Summer Stability: Solar forecasts are more accurate in summer due to predictable cloud patterns
  • MAPE Metrics: Wind MAPE typically 15-25%, Solar MAPE typically 10-18%
  • Seasonal Pattern: Forecast errors peak during seasonal transitions (spring/fall)
  • Ramp Events: Large forecast errors occur during rapid generation changes
