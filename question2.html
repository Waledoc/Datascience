❓ Question 2: How Does Generation Mix Shift on High Renewable Days?

Research Question

How does Germany's generation mix shift on days with high renewable output — and which conventional sources are reduced the most?

Interactive: Generation Mix Comparison

Impact on Conventional Sources

Percentage change in generation when renewable output is high

💡 Key Insights

  • Lignite Reduction: Lignite plants reduced by ~30-40% on high renewable days
  • Coal Impact: Hard coal reduced by ~25-35%
  • Gas Flexibility: Gas plants show smaller reductions due to flexibility
  • Nuclear Stability: Nuclear output remains relatively stable
  • Market Mechanism: Renewables (low marginal cost) displace expensive fossil fuels
